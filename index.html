I am currently a PhD student at the Institute of Mathematics of Bordeaux (IMB), in the team Image, Optimization, and Probabilities (IOP). My PhD thesis is under the supervision of Jean-Francois Aujol and Aude Rondepierre, and falls within the PEPR PDE-AI project funded by the ANR.

I am interested in studying momentum algorithms with a machine learning point of view. More broadly, I am particulary interested in links between machine learning and optimization.
